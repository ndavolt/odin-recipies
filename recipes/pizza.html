Pizza

Description

A delicious pizza thats so easy to make!

Your friends will marvel at your pizza making ability!

Ingredients:

  • Credit Card
  • Telephone or Computer

Steps:

  1. Google your favorite pizza place!
  2. If you are brave, call and place an order!
  3. If you are like most of us, place an order online. If you cannot order online, Google your second favorite pizza place!
  4. Hide the box and present the pizza as your own creation!
